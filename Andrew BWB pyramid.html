Work HR Slope per Step

bpm/s across the full 3-min work block · Lower = HR more stable across the block, less cardiac drift

BWB Plateau Ratio — (45-60s slope) / (0-15s slope)

Lower is better · 0.0 = HR has fully plateaued by 45s (recovery done early) · 1.0 = HR still dropping at end of minute · Negative = HR reversed direction
